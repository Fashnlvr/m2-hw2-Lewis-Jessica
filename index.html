Career Goals

While my goal is to continue working in my current field of Talent Acquisition, I would like to expand my career to become a recruitment marketing professional. This career goal is what attracted me to the Master of Arts in Mass Communication with a focus on Online Communication and Web Design. The completion of this graduate program in conjunction with my existing professional background will afford me the skillsets necessary to become a Digital Marketing Manager who advises businesses on implementing engaging recruitment marketing strategies that will allow them to attract the best talent.

My desire to acquire Web Design skills stems from the occurrences in current and previous positions where there was a need for someone who understood the basics of coding to continue meeting business demands. For instance, in one of my previous roles, I led the implementation of a new applicant tracking system. The purpose of this platform was to allow us to engage with candidates more effectively through a user-friendly website interface. However, we had to seek the help of a web designer to plug in the new features to meet our goal. The offering of strategic communication skills in conjunction with the ability to code would lend itself well to achieving the primary goal in Talent Acquisition, establishing a presence that enables the company to build a dynamic talent pipeline while promoting brand awareness to current and potential clients.
